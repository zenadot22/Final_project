About Kasi Artwork

Kasi Artwork is the world’s fastest growing online art gallery,
connecting people with art and the artist they love.
Kasi Artwork offers a beautiful selection of ballpoint drawings,
in various price ranges.
For a very small fee you select an art of your choice ,
and you get to communicate directly with the artist
Zwelakhe "Mr BigDreamer" Ngobese.
Our aim is to connect art lovers directly with the artists.
With Kasi Artwork we are redefining the experience of buying
and selling art by making it easy,
convenient and welcoming for art lovers and artists alike.
We are based in the Cape Town, internationally known for its rich art culture,
prominent design and expertise.
